health & wellness

Blue Label has a long history in developing health & wellness apps, it’s how we got our start. In 2012, we helped BabyMed, the web… leader in women’s fertility and pregnancy health, to bring their set of fertility and pregnancy tools to the iPhone. Since then, we’ve partnered with Columbia University to create MySmileBuddy, a set of iPad and iPhone apps to help promote childhood dental health and also helped to launch SuperBaby, a video guide to help parents with infant physiotherapy. Further, in 2015 we helped BabyMed bring to market the first set of fertility tools designed for Apple Watch.
